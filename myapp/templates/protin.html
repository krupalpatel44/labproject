C-Reactive Protein (CRP) Test Details

We offer the C-Reactive Protein (CRP) test to help diagnose and monitor inflammation in the body. The CRP test measures the level of C-reactive protein in your blood, which can indicate the presence of inflammation or infection.

  • Used to detect inflammation in the body
  • Helps diagnose conditions such as infections, autoimmune diseases, and chronic inflammatory diseases
  • Can be used to monitor the effectiveness of treatment for inflammation
  • And many more...
